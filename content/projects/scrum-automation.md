## Scrum Automation Toolkit

A collection of **Python utilities and shell scripts** designed to streamline common Scrum Master tasks and eliminate manual, repetitive work.

### Components

- **Sprint Report Generator** — Automated creation of comprehensive sprint retrospectives with burndown data
- **Jira Velocity Calculator** — Analyzes historical sprint data to predict capacity and identify trends
- **Backlog Grooming Script** — Bulk operations for story estimation, tagging, and organization
- **Release Notes Generator** — Pulls closed issues and generates formatted release notes
- **Stakeholder Notification System** — Automated alerts for sprint starts, completions, and blockers

### Impact

Reduced sprint administrative overhead by **3+ hours per sprint**, allowing the team to focus on delivery instead of ceremony logistics. Improved data-driven decision making by providing real-time velocity and capacity insights.

**Usage:** Scripts are modular and can be run standalone or integrated into your CI/CD pipeline.

**Status:** Actively maintained with new utilities added based on team feedback.
