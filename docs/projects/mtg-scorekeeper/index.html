MTG Scorekeeper

A clean, lightweight web app for tracking game state during Magic: The Gathering matches. Keeps tabs on life totals, poison counters, and other game metrics without the complexity of full arena clients.

Built from scratch to solve a specific problem: you need something fast and simple when you’re actively playing. No account required, no ads, just the essentials.
